"Teri muskurahat mein chhupi hai meri khushiyan, Tere saath bitaye har pal mein hai kuch khaas baat. Dil se dil mila, ishq mein hai raaz yeh pyara, Keh doon tujhse aaj, tu meri ho bas hamesha. Tere bina adhoori hai meri har khwahish, Teri aankhon mein hai meri zindagi ki disha. Kya tu bhi hoja meri, meri zindagi ka hissa? Bata de tu bhi, kya tu mera saath degi, miss? In Shayari ki zubaan mein chhupa hai mera pyaar, Muskura de tu, agar haan hai yaar. Dil se poochta hoon, mere liye hai kya tayaar, Keh do mujhe, tu bhi hai mere pyaar ka izhaar."
